The Industry Is Lying To You.

Certain platforms want to keep you dependent, slowly taking over your book of business. Here are the three massive lies they tell you about ICHRA to maintain their leverage.

01

"ICHRA is too complicated to do yourself."

02

"You need a TPA to handle quoting."

This is how they get between you and your client. If they control the quote, they control the relationship. A true agent platform gives you the live quoting engine directly, cutting out the middleman entirely.

03

"A Broker of Record letter is required."

This is the ultimate trap. When you sign over the BOR, you surrender your commissions and your client list. You should never have to sacrifice your AOR just to provide an ICHRA solution to a client.
