ICHRA Isn't Complicated.
It's Structured.

When broken into the right steps, and supported by the right infrastructure, it becomes not only manageable but incredibly efficient for you and your clients.

1

Employer Contribution Strategy

The employer decides exactly what they want to spend. Not with what a carrier dictates, but what fits their business bottom line perfectly.

2

Class Structure Design

Employees are grouped into customizable classes (full-time, part-time, geographic, etc.). This allows complete flexibility in how contributions are intelligently distributed.

3

Live Quoting & Plan Modeling

Plans are modeled instantly based on those contributions. This is where control matters most—because whoever runs the quote shapes the final outcome and protects the relationship.

4

Seamless Enrollment & Administration

Employees select the exact plans that fit their individual needs via a seamless platform. The broker stays involved for high-level support, renewals, and adjustments without dealing with administrative nightmares.
